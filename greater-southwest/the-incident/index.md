When the flash and the boom end, the airport is still standing—but the world outside is wrong. Radios work; cells do not. Runways end at river and rock instead of prairie. Leadership falls to whoever can keep the crowd from becoming a mob, then from becoming prey.

*TSA procedural realism meets portal displacement and political thriller; thirteen chapters to the end of this arc of the primer.*
