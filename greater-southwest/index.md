A near-future international hub—Greater Southwest International—whose people, planes, and politics vanish in an instant from the Texas plains and reappear somewhere impossible. Federal Security Director Isaac Jameson holds the line at the checkpoint while soldiers, airlines, and ten thousand strangers discover they are no longer in Kansas, or Dallas, or any map that still applies.

The chronicle begins with **The Greater Southwest Incident**: order under chaos, first contact without a playbook, and the slow realization that survival may mean becoming something the modern world was built to prevent.
