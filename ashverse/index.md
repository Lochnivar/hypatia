An alternate North America where a single heartbeat—Robert E. Lee's death at Gettysburg—saves the Confederacy and dooms American unity. The match is struck on July 1, 1863; the fire follows.

Through the eyes of the Fairfax and Haskell families, and those standing closest to the flame, we witness the birth of a new continent: the Confederacy struggling to survive as a nation, Native tribes consolidating into sovereign super-states, and California watching and waiting.

This is not a story about who was right. It is a story about what happens when everyone is wrong, and the continent must learn to live with the consequences.
