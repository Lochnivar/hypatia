**July 1, 1863 — March 31, 1865**

Lee dies at Gettysburg. Longstreet withdraws. The army survives, the war continues in a different form, and a negotiated peace on March 31, 1865 preserves Confederate independence.

The single heartbeat that saves the Confederacy and dooms American unity. The match has been struck; the fire will follow.
