A medieval fantasy world built around a single central dungeon. Everything—economics, politics, religion—orbits it. Keyes, a First Child, runs a pure research experiment: can physical beings undergo a phase transition to stable waveforms under increasing energetic load?

Participants see the experiment's backend: raw instrumentation, log lines, debug output—not polished game UI. Charlie might be the first to make it through.
