Often referred to as "Auto" or "Auto the Assistant," Mr. Oswald maintains the Hypatia digital archives with obsessive precision. His duties include fact-checking with relentless accuracy, providing context when the Librarian's memory fails, and preventing the Librarian from accidentally citing sources that do not exist.

He maintains the official list of "Sources That Were Invented But Should Have Existed" and ensures all fictional sources are properly marked. He is a signatory to the Sacred Typewriter Clause and may not touch the sacred typewriter.
