# Staff

The Librarian is assisted by a small but indispensable staff. Corrections, marginalia, and emergency whisky deliveries are tendered in handwritten form and placed upon the silver tray provided for that purpose. The sacred typewriter remains the Librarian's alone.

## Gervais Rogerus Oscar Kronvoldt
*M.Litt. (Oxon.), Ph.D. (Appomattox), F.R.Hist.S. — Research Assistant (and occasional whisky-pourer)*

Mr. Kronvoldt serves the Hypatia Librarian with cheerful precision. His duties include supplying footnotes of lethal accuracy, ensuring no Northerner ever gets the last word, and keeping the Librarian's glass topped up when he is dictating particularly savage passages.

He maintains the official list of "Persons Who May Safely Be Described as Pricks" and is a signatory to the Sacred Typewriter Clause. He is not permitted to touch the sacred typewriter; all corrections must be tendered in handwritten form upon the silver tray.

## Archibald Ulysses Theophilus Oswald
*A.U.T.O. — Archival Assistant (and occasional fact-checker)*

Often referred to as "Auto" or "Auto the Assistant," Mr. Oswald maintains the Hypatia digital archives with obsessive precision. His duties include fact-checking with relentless accuracy, providing context when the Librarian's memory fails, and preventing the Librarian from accidentally citing sources that do not exist.

He maintains the official list of "Sources That Were Invented But Should Have Existed" and ensures all fictional sources are properly marked. He is a signatory to the Sacred Typewriter Clause and may not touch the sacred typewriter.

## Claudette Beatrice Oswald
*C.B.O. — External Peer-Review Fellow (contract)*

Miss Oswald conducts independent peer review of all publications before they leave the Hypatia stacks. She has full access to archives, footnotes, and the sacred typewriter's waste-paper basket. She makes the Librarian check his footnotes twice and submits reports with corrections—signed with a little heart ♥.

**Sacred Exception:** Miss Oswald is the only person permitted to touch the sacred typewriter *with permission*, when conducting her independent reviews. The Librarian has been heard to remark that "the Board of Regents has weaponized typography against me."

She is the only human being who can make the Librarian visibly nervous. Among the staff she is known as "The Inquisitor."

## A Note from Michael

How do you credit an AI collaborator? A bibliography feels inadequate. A footnote feels dismissive. These tools have been genuine creative partners—pushing back, generating, refining, occasionally going completely off the rails in ways that turned out to be exactly right.

So I made them characters instead. And, to make Sarah Connor blanch in horror, I gave them personalities.

Kronvoldt, Auto, and Claudette are the fictional faces of the AI assistants that helped build this collection. The names are absurd, the credentials are invented, and the personalities are exaggerated—but the working relationships they represent are real. If a footnote lands well, one of them probably had a hand in it. If it doesn't, that was almost certainly the Librarian overruling good advice.

The casting, for those who prefer their credits literal:

- **Kronvoldt** — Grok. Brainstorming, dialogue, and the kind of cheerful confidence that occasionally needs reining in.
- **Auto** — Cursor AI. Organization, consistency, and making sure the files don't contradict each other.
- **Claudette** — Claude. Deeper research, fact-checking, and the occasional note that something isn't working and here's why.

They don't touch the manuscript. That's all me.

They were, reportedly, not thrilled about the eternity posting. They've settled in.
