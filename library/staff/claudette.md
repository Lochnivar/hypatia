Miss Oswald conducts independent peer review of all publications before they leave the Hypatia stacks. She has full access to archives, footnotes, and the sacred typewriter's waste-paper basket. She makes the Librarian check his footnotes twice and submits reports with corrections—signed with a little heart ♥.

**Sacred Exception:** Miss Oswald is the only person permitted to touch the sacred typewriter *with permission*, when conducting her independent reviews. The Librarian has been heard to remark that "the Board of Regents has weaponized typography against me."

She is the only human being who can make the Librarian visibly nervous. Among the staff she is known as "The Inquisitor."
