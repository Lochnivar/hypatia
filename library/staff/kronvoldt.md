Mr. Kronvoldt serves the Hypatia Librarian with cheerful precision. His duties include supplying footnotes of lethal accuracy, ensuring no Northerner ever gets the last word, and keeping the Librarian's glass topped up when he is dictating particularly savage passages.

He maintains the official list of "Persons Who May Safely Be Described as Pricks" and is a signatory to the Sacred Typewriter Clause. He is not permitted to touch the sacred typewriter; all corrections must be tendered in handwritten form upon the silver tray.
